Kitty Rescue - A Game Made Using Godot

Overview

Kitty Rescue is a game demo that I created while learning how to use Godot after making tutorial projects. In it, you literally go herd cats onto a specified area. Movement is done with the arrow keys or WASD. Later on, C is the interact key. The cats will follow you, but only if you stay within a certain range of them, otherwise the will wander off. You must get all the cats to the yellow circle to win the level. There are two tutorial levels, one where you just get the cats, and a second where you navigate around a maze like course. The third level adds a little more, you are told by your aunt to rescue the cats from an overgrown hedge maze and you are given an axe to cut down certain overgrown shrubs. Art assets are a combination of free, owned, and custom created or modified assets.
