Busy Bee Builders - Designing a Brand and Website for a New Company

Overview

Busy Bee Builders is a new company that wants to buy old houses, renovate or rebuild them, then sell them. They also have secondary and tertiary goals of acquiring or building rental mini-storage facilities and a small number of rental properties. I am acting as their brand and design consultant. This is an ongoing project that was started in December 2021. This page will detail the process so far. This is where I am prototyping this project: https://drfretnot.github.io/busy-bee-builders/
